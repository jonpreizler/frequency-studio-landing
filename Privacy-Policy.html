Privacy Policy

Frequency Studio
Frequency Studio is an offline-only application that prioritizes your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. It does not track user activity, and there is no advertising, in-app purchases, or requests for donations.

All app functionalities are processed locally on your device, and since no information is ever sent to us or any other party, there is no personal data to be shared or sold. The app contains no adult content, is suitable for all ages, and will never ask you for money or personal information.

Key Privacy Features:

No data collection or storage
No user activity tracking
No advertising or in-app purchases
All processing happens locally on your device
Suitable for all ages
Never requests money or personal information
Your privacy is our priority. This policy reflects our commitment to keeping your data completely private and secure.
